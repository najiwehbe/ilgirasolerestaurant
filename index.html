La Nostra Storia — Our Story

A Hidden Treasure
in South Florida

✦
"Inside, we stepped back into Italy. The service, the attention to the diners, was like finding a family."
— MarciaS, TripAdvisor

For over four decades, Il Girasole — the sunflower in Italian — has graced Delray Beach with an unwavering commitment to authentic Italian cuisine. Founded in 1980 and shaped by the same passionate chef since 1988, ours is a story written in garlic, olive oil, and love.

We believe fine dining should feel like visiting family. Every dish is prepared with the care and precision of generations of Italian tradition — from our hand-crafted pastas and delicate veal preparations to the freshest seafood from the day's catch. Come as a guest. Leave as family.

Ranked among the top 40 restaurants in Delray Beach and honored with a TripAdvisor Travelers' Choice distinction, Il Girasole is the destination of discerning diners who value authenticity above all else.
